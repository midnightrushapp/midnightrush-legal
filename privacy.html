MidnightRush Privacy Policy

MidnightRush respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the MidnightRush platform.

Information We Collect

We may collect account information, profile details, booking information, verification documents, payment-related identifiers, location data, push notification tokens, chat content, support requests, and emergency reports.

Identity Verification

MidnightRush may request identity verification information to improve platform safety, reduce fraud, and support moderation processes.

Payments

Payments and subscriptions are processed through Stripe. MidnightRush does not store full credit card information directly.

Location Services

Location information may be used during active bookings for safety features, live trip tracking, and emergency alerts.

Safety & Moderation

MidnightRush may review reports, emergency alerts, booking activity, and account behavior to maintain platform safety.

Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion inside the app. Certain records may be retained for fraud prevention, payment history, legal obligations, or dispute resolution.

Policy Updates

MidnightRush may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the platform means you agree to the latest version.
